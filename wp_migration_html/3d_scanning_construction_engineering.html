How it works

Matterport is set apart by its incredible ease. Get it for your job sites in 3 simple steps:

  1. Scan your job site

    If you can operate an iPad, you can operate a Matterport Pro Camera. Scanning 5,000 feet typically takes under an hour, and there is no leveling, alignment, or registration markers required.

  2. Upload your data

    Upload with one click, and our powerful vision pipeline automatically aligns and stitches your 2D and 3D data into a complete textured mesh that looks as real as being on-site.

  3. Annotate, measure, distribute

    Our in-browser tools let you tag points in your model with notes or more information and take measurements with ease. With our WebGL-based player, anyone can view and interact with 3D Spaces right from their rowser. Share with stakeholders via a simple link.
